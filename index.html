How it works

Simple setup, powerful results

Pilot runs on your Mac or a cloud server, wired into your MIT accounts — and answers wherever you are.

1

Deploy anywhere

One script provisions Pilot on your Mac or a cloud server with all MIT integrations wired up automatically.

2

Connect your MIT accounts

Link Canvas, email, and Piazza once. Pilot uses a persistent browser session to handle MIT SSO and Duo seamlessly.

3

Chat on Telegram

Message from anywhere via Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, and more. Pilot reads your courses, checks your email, queues print jobs — all in plain language.

Features

Built for life at MIT

Every integration is tailored to MIT's specific systems — not generic wrappers.

📚

Canvas & Piazza

Instantly surface assignments, announcements, and discussion posts from your active courses — no portal hunting.

✉️

MIT Email

Check and summarize your inbox via Apple Mail or Outlook, without leaving the Telegram conversation.

🖨️

MIT Printing

Send files to MobilePrint or a configured Pharos queue. Finds available printers dynamically across campus.

🔒

VPN & SSO

GlobalProtect VPN managed automatically. MIT SSO sessions reused so you skip repeated Touchstone and Duo prompts.

🔬

Multimodal Understanding

Send a photo, screenshot, or PDF. Pilot reads figures, summarizes papers, extracts data from slides, and answers questions about what it sees.

💬

On-demand & scheduled

Ask anything via Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, and more — or set recurring automations. Daily digests, deadline reminders, and real-time answers all arrive in your chat.
